What is BitLife?

BitLife, also known as BitLife Life Simulator, is a text-based simulation game that allows players to control a character's life from birth to death. Developed by Candywriter, the game presents real-life scenarios and choices, making it an engaging and highly interactive experience.

Each playthrough is unique, as every choice affects your character’s future, leading to different outcomes based on decisions related to education, relationships, careers, and more.

How to Play BitLife?

Playing BitLife is simple yet offers deep strategic choices:

  • Start Your Life: Begin with a randomly generated character, including name, nationality, and gender.
  • Make Life Choices: Decide on education, career, friendships, and relationships as your character grows.
  • Overcome Challenges: Face events such as job interviews, financial struggles, breakups, and family issues.
  • Shape Your Future: Choose whether to live a successful life, become a criminal, or take unexpected paths.
  • Restart and Experiment: Every new life presents a fresh opportunity to explore different choices and outcomes.

Each choice impacts your virtual life, making every playthrough a new adventure.
