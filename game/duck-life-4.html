Duck Life 4 - The Ultimate Duck Racing Adventure

What is Duck Life 4?

Duck Life 4 is an exciting duck racing game set in a world where genetically modified ducks have been banned. You train your duck team and compete in six different locations to defeat the reigning champion, Frank the Fire Duck. The game offers new challenges, improved gameplay, and a rich sense of progression.

Duck Life 4 Gameplay

The game starts with you selecting your first duck and training it to become a champion racer. You can later expand your team by purchasing new ducks with unique strengths. Each duck is trained in various skills, including running, flying, climbing, and swimming, to improve its performance in races.
